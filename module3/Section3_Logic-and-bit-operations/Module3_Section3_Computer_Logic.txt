Computer Logic

and => conjuction
   --Binary Operator
   --Priority that is lower than the one expressed by the comparison operators
   
or => disjunction
   --binary Operator
   --lower priority than "and"

not => turns truth into
   --unary operator performing a logical negation

Bitwise operators
    & (ampersand) - bitwise conjunction;
    | (bar) - bitwise disjunction;
    ~ (tilde) - bitwise negation;
    ^ (caret) - bitwise exclusive or (xor).
